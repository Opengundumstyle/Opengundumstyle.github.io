Loki

This is him, kicking up sands and rocks, having fun as long as he's outdoor running without a leash. I can't help but wonder how he has so much energy? Because if I just let him run, he would not stop, for a fact.

Unlike others who seen dog as one of their family members, I see loki as my mentor. He had taught me something that I could've never known by myself. It's the spirit of being happy and excited unconditionally regardless of whatever we do the day. Whether it was a relax walk by the bay, long climb to a hilly mountain or being stuck at home when it was a rainny day, he would be happy all along.
